About Us

Pt. Tilak Bhanu Sharma School, Firozabad It is a co-educational institution open to children of all communities. It follows the syllabus of NCERT New Delhi and medium of instruction is Hindi and English. At present there are 1000 students and 25 teachers, along with 5 non-teaching staff. At present the school has become a full-fledged institution with classes upto the secondary level.

We strive to inculcate in students a good philosophy of life based on faith in God, Respect for the individual, concern for the less privileged in society, and ideals of truth and justice.

From Manager's Desk

While keeping an eye on the academic pursuits and achievements of our children, we ensure that we give them good grounding in character building too. Good human virtues and moral values will help them cope with any harmful or negative experience that they may have from their surroundings. The present age is undergoing a change in values and mindsets. Gone are the days of stereotyped notions and values. The child of today is busy in exploring and would rather experience than follow blindly. We are proud of educating hundreds of children and giving them courage to live up to the ideals being taught to them. In the past years, the progress that we have made as a school, in spite of obvious hurdles and challenges, amazes everyone. Thanks to our hard working students, supportive parents and dedicated teachers. Let’s continue to work together and support each other. Let’s maintain sincerity and regularity in our efforts to impart quality and holistic education to the future policy makers of the world.

From Principal's Desk

Dear Parents,

‘Teaching is not a service, profession or a job. It is a pillar of the society.”

I am very fortunate to work with such dedicated, innovative and caring staff members, parents and students. I find each day an adventure filled with new experiences, learning for all, and a chance to constantly widen our horizons. It always gives me a great pleasure to see the sea of smiling faces of children and I must admit that on many days it is the students who make my day a little brighter. At Pt. Tilak Bhanu Sharma School, the growth of each individual student is our focus. We recognize that as we 'work together and learn together' students will be best able to achieve their potential.
