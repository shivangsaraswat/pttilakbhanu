From Manager's Desk

Pt. Tilak Bhanu Sharma School

While keeping an eye on the academic pursuits and achievements of our children, we ensure that we give them good grounding in character building too. Good human virtues and moral values will help them cope with any harmful or negative experience that they may have from their surroundings. The present age is undergoing a change in values and mindsets. Gone are the days of stereotyped notions and values. The child of today is busy in exploring and would rather experience than follow blindly. We are proud of educating hundreds of children and giving them courage to live up to the ideals being taught to them. In the past years, the progress that we have made as a school, in spite of obvious hurdles and challenges, amazes everyone. Thanks to our hard working students, supportive parents and dedicated teachers. Let’s continue to work together and support each other. Let’s maintain sincerity and regularity in our efforts to impart quality and holistic education to the future policy makers of the world.

Mr. Rajkumar Saraswat
(Manager)

About us

Pt. Tilak Bhanu Sharma School, Firozabad It is a co-educational institution open to children of all communities. It follows the syllabus of NCERT New Delhi and medium of instruction is Hindi and English. At present there are 1000 students and 25 teachers, along with 5 non-teaching staff. .
